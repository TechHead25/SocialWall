Anveshana AI Event FAQ

Q: What is Anveshana?
A: Anveshana is a tech-event platform that combines AI experiences like a chatbot, AI photo booth, social wall, and auto-generated highlights.

Q: Where is the help desk?
A: The help desk is near Hall B entrance.

Q: What are keynote timings?
A: Keynotes start at 10:00 AM and 4:00 PM.

Q: How do I join workshops?
A: Scan the QR on your event badge and pick a workshop slot.
